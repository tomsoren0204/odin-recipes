Wood Fired Pizza From Italy

Below is a recipe for wood fired pizza, take a slice of Italy to your home.

Ingredients

  • 625 grams bread flour
  • 18 grams kosher salt
  • 2 grams instant yeast
  • 375 grams water

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients together, knead.
  2. Add desired toppings, put in wood fired oven until crust is golden brown and toppings melted evenly
